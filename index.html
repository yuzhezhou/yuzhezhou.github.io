Yo!

I am a final-year master student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Purdue University. Currently, I am working with Prof. Goldenstein on a research project for developing a single-ended laser-based sensor in The Goldenstein Group. My thesis will focus on laser-absorption spectroscopy diagnostics (Scan-WMS) for diesel engines. Besides, I am also interested in engine performance analysis, electric vehicle and sensor technology for self-driving systems including Radar, camera, GPS and LiDAR.

In the last fall semester, I worked as a Thermal and Fluid Science Engineer Co-op in Cummins, Seymour, Power System BU. My main task was to perform 1D & 3D CFD analysis for high-horse power engines and provide related analsyis support for the designs.
